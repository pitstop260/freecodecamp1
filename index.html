ROHIT SHARMA

The hit-man sharma
ONLY man in the world to score Three double centuries in ODI's

Rohit Sharma Raising His Bat To Celebrate His century.

Here's a time line of ROHIT SHARMA:


  • 1987-Born in nagpur, India

  • 2006-Makes debut for india U-19 team during U-19 cricket world cup.

  • 2007-Makes debut for indian international ODI team (ODI Lasts upto 8hrs and each
    team gets 50 overs    ) against Ireland.

  • 2007-Makes debut for indian international T20I team (T20I Lasts upto 4hrs and each
    team gets 20 overs    ) against England.

  • 2011-Gets a 2 million USD/year contract from IPL franchise Mumbai indians.

  • 2013-Makes debut for indian international TEST team (TEST Played over 5 days
    and each team gets 2 innings and unlimited overs. 1 day in test lasts for 6hrs    ) against
    West indies.In the same he went on to score his 1st double century (209) against australian
    ODI team.

  • 2014-Makes a record of highest individual score 264 in ODI's during a match against
    Sri lankan ODI team.

  • 2015-Makes his first appearance in world cup for indian ODI team.

  • 2017-Makes his 3rd double century (208 not out) in ODI's against srilanka

  • 2019-He became the first batsman to score five centuries in the same World Cup tournament.
    Also becomes vice-captain of indian team.

Life

Rohit sharma with his wife

Sharma married Ritika Sajdeh in December 2015.They have a daughter, born in December 2018.
Sharma is noted for his philanthropic activities and promotes various causes such as animal welfare, health, and children.
He is particularly vocal about the protection of animals and has supported various initiatives and organisations to promote the cause.
Sharma has been sponsored by several brands including the Swiss watchmaker Hublot and CEAT.
In his career spanning 11 years, Sharma has endorsed many other brands including Maggi, Fair and Lovely, Lay's, Nissan,
energy drink Relentless, Nasivion nasal spray, Aristocrat by VIP Industries, Adidas and Oppo mobiles.
